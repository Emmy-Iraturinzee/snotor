Real Salary

Real Salary revolves around a weekly manual procedure that involves extracting and refining salary data from diverse job sites. With this app, users can gain access to salary information across the entire database by making a one-time payment.

Type of software: Web App

Scope of work: Front-end, Back-end, UI/UX Design, Project Management, Quality Assurance

The project aims to meet the increasing demand for transparent and accessible salary data in the job market. It focuses on providing accurate and reliable data through a manual extraction and cleaning process, ensuring the highest data quality

This manual approach is critical for establishing a strong trust and credibility with the user base, which is necessary for the project’s initial growth and future scaling.

What our client
wanted

Amy Shratter

Senior Recruitment Consultant

We needed to develop an app that helps users quickly and in one place view all salary offers for vacancies of interest in the selected state and city.

Challenge

  • Setting up a system in a short time period to create and handle special access codes after payment, making sure that paid content is accessed securely and exclusively.

Key features

  • Development of a website with advanced filtering options (by state, city, job type, etc.) that is intuitive and user-friendly.
  • Successful integration of Stripe for secure and efficient payment processing, with a seamless user experience from pre-checkout to post-checkout stages.
  • Generating a substantial user base that engages with both the free and paid versions of the service.
  • Establishment of a process for generating and managing unique access codes post-payment, ensuring secure and exclusive access to paid content.
