How it works

  • Discovery stage
  • Design&Prototyping stage
  • Development stage
  • Evolution stage

Discovery stage

  • this is when we learn all the details about your project through an IT-consultation;
  • we conduct first approximate estimate of the project after market studies, industry, and your business analysis;
  • we analyze your business needs, goals, pain points, and risks and create a detailed plan for further work;
  • we make a feasibility study of your idea;
  • we choose the optimal models of pricing and workflow for your project;
  • we also create a full strategy that helps to determine the long- and short-term goals.

Design&Prototyping stage

  • at this stage we determine all must-have features for the project;
  • we estimate the costs of the development process, approve it with you, and then proceed;
  • based on your requirements, we create a fitting prototype;
  • we create a prototype that is legally your property and can be developed into a full-fledged IT-product;
  • the prototype you’ll get by the end of this stage is polished until it is a full embodiment of your ideas;
  • we create detailed SRS (system requirements specification) and WBS (work breakdown structure) documentation based on the prototype.

Development stage

  • before the development itself begins, we create the app’s architecture, which can be upscaled and evolved in the future;
  • we work on UI|UX design to create software with maximum usability;
  • we create the code itself using the most suitable technologies;
  • our QA-engineers test the software on each round of development;
  • all data we work with is stored on secured servers and protected by encryption and NDA;
  • we create a reliable and detailed documentation pack that you can work with further;
  • our team is also ready to teach your staff how to work with the new software.

Evolution stage

  • our business analyst creates a presentation on all software product capabilities;
  • we provide different types of maintenance suited for all goals: malfunctions correction and prevention, software improvement, and others;
  • if any errors occur, we’ll fix them as soon as possible and at our expense;
  • we take your analytical materials into account if you have it;
  • we analyze the latest news and trends of your market segment to make your software relevant and based on it we create recommendations to improve the existing software;
  • we decide after consulting with you whether you need new features or an update for your software.
