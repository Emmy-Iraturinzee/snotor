MyScore

SaaS platform for convenient generation and management of credit data

About

Location: Oslo, Norway

Industry: Fintech

Budget: $10,000 to $49,999

Product link: myscore.no

Type of software: Web App (SaaS)

Scope of work: Back-end, Front-end, UI/UX Design, Project Management, Quality Assurance

MyScore is a CRM platform that provides small and medium-sized companies with detailed PDF reports on their credit situation. These reports can be used in private and public tenders, bidding processes, or as a visual representation of the company’s current financial situation.
Everything started when one of our clients from another fintech project – Defero – recommended us to the MyScore startup. We’re passionate about fintech projects and our future clients were looking for a reliable tech partner. All the stars aligned for our cooperation!
MyScore was a newly established company at that time. Their core idea was to create software that is meant to show if the company is financially stable and attractive to investors.
The main goal was to arrange the huge amount of financial information in a clear structure and understandable design, making it easy to perceive for all types of players towards B2B and B2C.

Key Features

  • Generation of the PDF report with the detailed credit information of a company
  • Ability to save the report in the user’s profile for a set period of time
  • System for daily updates of the report
  • Ability to put an official logo badge with the company’s actual rating and score on its website
  • Notification system that enables to receive detailed info on Payment Remark changes for a specific company via email and user profile
